# EterNox Security Policy

## Security Commitment

EterNox is committed to promoting security awareness, responsible blockchain participation, and transparent ecosystem development.

While blockchain technology creates new opportunities, it also introduces risks such as phishing attacks, malicious smart contracts, wallet compromises, and social engineering attempts.

The EterNox ecosystem encourages all community members to prioritize security when interacting with blockchain applications.

---

## User Security Guidelines

To help protect your assets:

### Wallet Security

* Never share your private key.
* Never share your seed phrase.
* Store backup phrases securely offline.
* Use hardware wallets whenever possible.

### Website Verification

Always verify that you are using official EterNox resources.

Official channels:

* Website: https://eternox.org
* GitHub: https://github.com/EterNox
* X (Twitter): https://x.com/EterNox01
* Telegram: https://t.me/eternox1

---

## Smart Contract Safety

Before interacting with any smart contract:

* Verify contract addresses through official channels.
* Review contract permissions.
* Avoid connecting wallets to unknown applications.
* Research projects thoroughly before participating.

---

## Scam Awareness

Common threats include:

* Fake airdrops
* Impersonation accounts
* Phishing websites
* Malicious wallet approvals
* Social engineering attacks

Community members should remain cautious and verify information through official EterNox channels.

---

## Responsible Disclosure

If a security issue affecting EterNox infrastructure is discovered, community members are encouraged to report it responsibly.

Reports should include:

* Description of the issue
* Reproduction steps
* Potential impact
* Suggested mitigation (if available)

---

## Future Security Initiatives

As the EterNox ecosystem grows, future initiatives may include:

* Security education content
* Project risk awareness resources
* Security-focused ecosystem tools
* Community best-practice guides

---

## Disclaimer

EterNox cannot guarantee protection against all risks associated with blockchain technologies.

Users remain responsible for securing their wallets, devices, and accounts.

Always conduct independent research and exercise caution when interacting with digital assets.
