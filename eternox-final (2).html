How It Works

Simple mechanics. Real yield.

Eternox turns DEX trading fees into a transparent, on-chain revenue stream distributed to $NOX holders every epoch.

01
🔄

Traders Swap on Eternox DEX

Every token swap on the Eternox DEX generates a 0.3% fee, collected directly in the traded pair's tokens — no middleman, no delay.

02
🏦

Fees Flow to the Treasury

70% of all collected fees route automatically to the protocol treasury smart contract. 30% goes to liquidity providers as incentive.

03
💰

$NOX Holders Earn Pro-Rata

Every 7-day epoch, the treasury distributes accumulated fees to $NOX stakers, proportional to their share. Claim anytime after each epoch.

04
🗳️

Govern the Protocol

$NOX holders vote on fee tiers, new trading pairs, treasury allocations, and protocol upgrades. One token, one vote.

05
🔥

Buyback & Burn

10% of weekly fees are used to buy $NOX on the open market and permanently burn them — reducing supply and supporting long-term value.

06
📈

More Volume = More Yield

As Eternox DEX grows, trading volume increases, fees increase, and yield for $NOX holders increases. Growth is shared, not captured.
