# Executive Summary

EterNox is a community-driven blockchain ecosystem powered by the NOX token. The project aims to create a transparent and sustainable environment where users can participate in ecosystem growth, governance, and future utility services.

NOX serves as the native utility token of the EterNox ecosystem and is designed to support long-term community engagement and ecosystem development.

---

# Problem Statement

The cryptocurrency market contains thousands of tokens with limited utility and short-term focus. Many projects struggle to build lasting communities and sustainable ecosystems.

EterNox seeks to address these challenges by focusing on transparency, community participation, and gradual ecosystem expansion.
